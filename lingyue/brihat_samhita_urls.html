Brihat-samhita [sanskrit]

26,560 words

Hinduism Jyotisha Sanskrit
Buy now!

Summary: The Sanskrit text of the Brihat-samhita from the 6th-century authored by Varaha Mihira in present-day Ujjain, India. It primarily deals with astrology and astronomy but is presented as an encyclopedia of knowledge.
